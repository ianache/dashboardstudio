  <action>
Create `backend/app/api/endpoints/ml_models.py`:
1. `POST /`: Upload `.pkl`. Restrict to `admin/designer`.

   - Save metadata to `MLModel` table.

4. `DELETE /{id}`: Delete from DB and disk. Restrict to `admin/designer`.
5. Mount in `backend/app/api/router.py` as `/ml-models`.
